How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own timed trials or lab tests. Instead, our recommendations come from combing through decades of player feedback on forums, long-term owner reviews, and expert evaluations published by coaches and sports-equipment analysts. We look at what actually holds up season after season – stitching that doesn’t pop, leather that doesn’t dry out, and patterns that consistently perform for specific positions. We also track common failure points: broken laces around the thumb and pinky, hardened leather in the palm, and warped finger stalls. Only gloves with a proven track record of surviving heavy use make our shortlist. Every pick here has been worn by high school, college, and professional players for years, giving us confidence that they’ll perform for you too.
